# Website Copywriting Skill Source Map

Research date: 2026-06-20.

This skill was derived from a dedicated website-copywriting research corpus.

## Core sources

### Robert Cialdini — Influence / persuasion principles

- Influence at Work — Seven Principles of Persuasion: https://www.influenceatwork.com/7-principles-of-persuasion/
- Influence at Work — Books and Publications: https://www.influenceatwork.com/books-and-publications/
- Cialdini Institute — About / principles + code of ethics: https://cialdini.com/about
- Cialdini Institute — Certified Practitioner module descriptions: https://cialdini.com/certified-practitioner

Skill contribution: ethical persuasion, reciprocity, liking, social proof, authority, scarcity, consistency, unity.

### Claude Hopkins — Scientific Advertising

- Public web text index: https://scientificadvertising.blogspot.com/2006/02/contents.html
- Emphasized chapters: advertising laws, salesmanship, offer service, mail-order accountability, headlines, psychology, specificity, full story, research, samples, test campaigns, negative advertising, letter writing, good business.

Skill contribution: measurable salesmanship, specificity, testing, samples, research, headline-as-selector.

### David Ogilvy — old-school advertising/copywriting

- Ogilvy official page: https://www.ogilvy.com/ideas/ogilvy-75-quotations-david-ogilvy
- Official PDF linked from that page: `Quotations_of_David_Ogilvy_v2.pdf`, consulted for short source-quote fragments only.
- Oliver Palmer on Ogilvy’s foreword to Scientific Advertising: https://www.oliverpalmer.com/blog/david-ogilvy-foreword-to-scentific-advertising/

Skill contribution: sell-or-else, big idea, one simple promise, reader respect, testing, content over decoration, brand personality.

### Eugene Schwartz — Breakthrough Advertising

- Official Breakthrough Advertising site — Five States of Awareness: https://breakthroughadvertisingbook.com/the-five-states-of-awareness-the-key-to-marketing-that-actually-works/
- Rob Palmer — Schwartz for modern funnels: https://robpalmer.com/blog/eugene-schwartz-breakthrough-advertising-lessons
- NordicCopy — Market Sophistication: https://nordiccopy.com/market-sophistication/

Skill contribution: awareness levels, market sophistication, mass desire, mechanism, message-market fit, page length follows awareness gap.

## Copyright handling

- Do not include copyrighted book text in generated outputs.
- Use summaries, frameworks, and short quote fragments only.
- Do not use pirated book PDFs as source evidence.
- Do not invent page numbers unless an authorized copy has been consulted.
