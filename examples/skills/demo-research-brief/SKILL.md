# Example: Demo Research Brief

> **Example skill:** This bundle exists to demonstrate Open SSR. It is intentionally small and is not presented as a production research methodology.

Use it to test the complete registry path:

1. List or search for `demo-research-brief`.
2. Describe its registry metadata.
3. Retrieve `SKILL.md` and `templates/brief.md` with checksums.
4. Install it into a configured scratch skills directory.
5. Confirm the installed files remain inside that directory.

## Example workflow

1. Identify a topic and audience.
2. Collect public source notes.
3. Summarize the key claims.
4. Flag uncertainty and missing evidence.
5. Fill in `templates/brief.md` with a concise brief and next steps.

## Completion criterion

The example is complete when a fresh client can retrieve both files, verify their checksums, install them into a scratch skills root, and read the installed `SKILL.md`.
